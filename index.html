Brand Story

Kifaru Roasters was founded by Kiweewa Farouk, inspired by the strength, resilience, and unwavering dedication of his mother, Nakayima Harriet.

From her tireless work and nurturing spirit came the values that guide us today — hard work, authenticity, dignity, and pride in our roots. Just as the rhino (Kifaru) stands as a symbol of power and protection, Nakayima’s legacy is the backbone of our journey.

"We don’t just make coffee. We craft heritage."

Born in Africa, Kifaru Roasters embodies the rich soil, bold spirit, and warm heart of our continent. Every bean tells a story of family, resilience, and the relentless pursuit of excellence by the people who cultivate it.
